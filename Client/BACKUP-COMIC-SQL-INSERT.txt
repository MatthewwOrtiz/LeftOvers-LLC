The Wallers and the Squad remain holed up inside a redbrick awaiting Rustam to launch his attack, inside Waller remains cold and aloof to her children who attempt to reach out to her. They remind her of her deceased husband and children but Waller is alerted to a man lurking outside, believing him to be Rustam they release Croc who refuses to stand down and receives an electrical shock.

The man is revealed to be a courier carrying a tablet with a message from Rustam. He reveals that he tracked down her children to reunite them, for that is his revenge. He mocks her for not knowing that one of them is going to have a child but she is pushed away by Coretta who denies Waller as her mother and the children leave in tears. Waller stands alone, her victory empty and alone.
